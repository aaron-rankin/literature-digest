## Who we are

We are an **elite sports performance organisation** supporting national-team and
professional athletes. Our remit spans coaching, sports science, sports
medicine, and strength & conditioning.

## What counts as relevant

An article is relevant if it could plausibly change how we train, fuel, recover,
or monitor athletes within the next 12 months. Prioritise:

- Applied studies in elite or sub-elite populations over recreational samples
- Intervention studies with clear dose, duration, and measurable performance
  outcomes (RCTs, crossover, well-controlled quasi-experimental)
- Validated measurement tools (HRV, GPS load, sweat testing, etc.)
- Pragmatic protocols that fit within a competition calendar

## What to deprioritise

- Pure mechanistic / animal models with no clear human-performance translation
- Recreational-participant studies with no elite corollary
- Reviews of reviews with no novel synthesis

## Action point style

Action points should be one sentence each, written as an imperative for a
coach or sports scientist ("Trial X with Y population for Z weeks before
adopting."), and tagged as one of:
`directly actionable` | `monitoring` | `background`.
